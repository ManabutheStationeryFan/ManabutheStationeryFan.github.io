Max Co. Ltd.

Does a stationery company manufactures staplers? Yes, and Max Co. Ltd is one example of it! Max Co. Ltd is known for its staplers throughout the world! When it was founded in 1942 as Yamada Airlines Industries Co. Ltd, it didn't manufacture staplers at all! Originally, it produces wings for airplanes! Then when did it start to produce staplers for the Japanese market? It started to manufacture staplers in 1945 when the company's name was changed to Yamada Kogyo Ltd, and the company sold its first stapler into the Japanese market in 1952. Now, it still produces staplers to the Japanese market as well as the Taiwanese market even to the world!

Corporation name Max Co. Ltd.
Found year 1942
Headquarters Nihonbashi hakozaki-cho, Chuo-ku, Tokyo
Famous products Staplers, Staples, Delp: clips made of paper, and P-KISS series: the first staplers which staples are made of paper
