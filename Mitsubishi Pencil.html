Mitsubishi Pencil Co. Ltd.
Mitsubishi Pencil is the oldest Japanese stationary manufacturer in Japan. Founded in 1886, it's originally called Masaki Pencil Manufacture Co. Its name was changed to Mitsubishi Pencil in 1952. It's headquartered in Shinagawa, Tokyo.
Corporation name Mitsubishi Pencil Co. Ltd
Found year 1886
Headquarters Shinagawa, Tokyo, Japan
Famous products Kuru Toga: keep leads sharp pencils, Alpha Gel: soft grip oil-based pens and sharp pencils, Signo gel-ink pens, and Jetstream oil-based pens
