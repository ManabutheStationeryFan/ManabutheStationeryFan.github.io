Pentel Co. Ltd
Pentel Co. ltd. is also a major manufacturer for pens and correction tapes. Some major differences from other manufacturers is that the company also produces correction pens, correction fluids, digital stationeries, white-board markers, and sign pens. Founded in 1946, it was originally called the Japan Stationery Co. Ltd. (Dai Nihonbungu kabushikigaisha) It changed into its current name in 1971. Now, it has 21 branch offices wordwide, including Taiwan, France, Italy, U. S., Canada, and more.
Corporation name Pentel Co. Ltd.
Found year 1946
Headquarters Chuo-ku, Tokyo, Japan
Famous products Sign pen, Fude pens, Energel: quick dry gell ink pens, Knockle: back push and ink refill white-board markers, Correction fluids, Correction pens, Vicuna: smooth oil-based ball point pens, and Orenz: the first 0.2mm sharp pencils
