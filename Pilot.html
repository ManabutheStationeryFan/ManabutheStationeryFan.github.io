Pilot Corp.
Pilot was founded in 1918 by Ryosuke Namiki. Originally called the Namiki Manufacturing Company, its name was hanged to Pilot Fountain Pens in 1938 and to Pilot in 1989. It's then changed to Pilot Corporation, the present name, in 2003. But although it's a stationary company, it also produces toys for kids.
Corporation name Pilot Corporation
Found year 1918
Headquarters Chuo-ku, Tokyo, Japan
Famous products Capless: the first capless fountain pen, Fure fure sharp pencils: with shake mechanism, Acroball series oil-based pens, and Dr. Grip series oil-based pens and sharp pencils
