Platinum Fountain Pens

Platinum Fountain Pens Inc. is the FIRST manufacturer to produce fountain pens in Japan. It is founded by a fountain pen importer Nakada Shunichi in 1919. Do you ever think that Platinum Fountain Pens manufactures fountain pens with actual nib made of platinum? Yes they do! In 1966, the company released a fountain pen named "Platinum Platinum," which had actual nib made of platinum! Now, they realeased so many products, including the world's first unbreakable sharp pencil!

Corporation name Platinum Fountain Pens Inc.
Found year 1919
Headquarters Higashi-Ueno, Taito-ku, Tokyo
Famous products Fountain pens, Preppy: the first 0.3 fountain pen, and Oleenu: world's first unbreakable sharp pencil
