Stationeries!

Do you think that stationeries from Japan can be bought home? Yes, it could and it is a new kind of Japanese souvenirs apart from cosmetics and foods! You can buy a lot of stationeries in stationery stores like Tokyu Hands. So try to buy some stationeries in Japan afterwards and don't spend a lot of money on cosmetic products!!! :P

Where can you buy Japanese stationeries?
  • Tokyu Hands*
  • Yodobashi Camera
  • Other stationery stores (which can be found at shopping malls.)
* Now, you can go to Tokyu Hands stores without going to Japan! In Taiwan, there is a branch of Tokyu Hands called Hands Tailung. It opened throughout malls in Taiwan, including Kaohsiung's Hanshin Arena, Tainan's Ximen Shinkong Mitsukoshi and more.
