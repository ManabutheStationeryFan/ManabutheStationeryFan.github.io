Zebra Corp.
Zebra was founded as Ishikawa Pen Manufacture Company in 1896. It was changed to Zebra Co. Ltd, the present name, in 1963. It is the first manufacturer to produce ball-point pens in Japan The first ball-point pen in Japan, No. 5911, went on sale at 1959. Now, it still produces ball-point pens for Japanese people.
Corporation name Zebra Co. Ltd
Found year 1896
Headquarters Shinjuku, Tokyo, Japan
Famous products Sarasa: gel-ink pens, Surari: emulsion ink pens (oil-based ink plus gel ink), Sharbo: the first multi-purpose pen, and DelGuard, its first unbreakable sharp pencil*
